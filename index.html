About Us

Lal Bahadur Arts, Science and S. B. Solabanna Shetty Commerce College, Sagara, is dedicated to
academic excellence, offering a wide range of undergraduate and postgraduate programs. Our faculty is passionate
about teaching, and the campus provides a dynamic environment for both academic and personal growth. We also place a significant
emphasis on student support services, including scholarships, extracurricular activities, and career development.
